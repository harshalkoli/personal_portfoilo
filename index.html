Get to know me!

I am a full-stack web developer with a passion for creating functional and visually appealing websites. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I strive to deliver high-quality, user-friendly solutions that meet the needs of clients and users alike.

In my journey as a developer, I have honed my skills in various technologies and frameworks. On the front-end, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I have experience with popular libraries and frameworks like React and Angular. I enjoy crafting responsive and intuitive user interfaces that enhance the overall user experience.

On the back-end, I have expertise in server-side programming languages such as Node.js and Python, as well as frameworks like Express.js and Django. I am adept at building RESTful APIs and integrating them with databases such as MySQL and MongoDB. Security and scalability are always at the forefront of my mind when designing and implementing back-end systems.
